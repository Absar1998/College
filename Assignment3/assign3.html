Impressively Big. Impossibly Thin.

The new 15-inch MacBook Air makes room for more of what you love with a spacious Liquid Retina display. Supercharged by the M2 chip - and with up to 18 hours of battery life. Delivering blazing-fast performance in an ultraportable design.

The M2 chip brings more speed to everything you do - whether you're editing a video for class, collaborating on a business plan or stream a show while you shop online. And with all-day battery life, you can leave the power adapter at home.

Compared to a PC laptop with an Intel Core i7 processor, MacBook Air delivers up to 2x faster performance, up to 50 percent faster web browsing and up to 50 percent longer battery life.
